A Little About Me

I was born and raised in Detroit, MI by my very Jamaican parents. Being the first first-generation child of my parents, the love for learning was instilled in me from day one (along with a set of superstitious beliefs that will either intrigue you or make you question my judgement). Along with learning, I love most things science fiction, anime, my offbeat humor, and naps. I'm currently enrolled in Perficient's Bright Paths program and hope to transition from an IT support role to a software engineering role. After securing a software engineering role, I would love to pay it forward by mentoring other non-traditional candidates that want to break into tech.
